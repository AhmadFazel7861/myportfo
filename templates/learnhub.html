003 : LearnHub

LearnHub is a comprehensive online education platform built with Django and React. It allows instructors to create and manage interactive courses with video lectures, quizzes, downloadable resources, and discussion boards. Students can enroll in courses, track progress, earn certificates, and interact through forums. Admin features include instructor verification, category management, and analytics dashboards.

"LearnHub brings together instructors and learners with structured content, real-time discussions, and a certificate-based learning model."

— LearnHub
