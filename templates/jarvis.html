004 : Jarvis

Jarvis is an intelligent virtual assistant built using Python and integrated with natural language processing, text-to-speech, and speech recognition technologies. Jarvis can perform a variety of tasks like setting reminders, reading emails, fetching real-time weather and news, managing files, opening applications, and responding to voice commands. It’s designed for desktop use and acts as a productivity booster with a conversational interface.
